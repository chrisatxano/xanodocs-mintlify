Verified non-profits are eligible to receive a 50% discount for our [LAUNCH](https://www.xano.com/pricing/)or SCALE plan . You will need to provide your company EIN number or 501(c)(3) status by the IRS.

For non-U.S. based non profits, we would need proof of qualifications, registrations, or other filings to be considered.

You can apply [here](https://go.xano.co/non-profit-discount-form).

<Info>
This pricing is subject to change, and only valid for as long as you retain your non-profit status. Xano reserves the right to verify your status at any time.

</Info>
